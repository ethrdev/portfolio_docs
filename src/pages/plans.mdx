# Plans

Our application offers three distinct plans tailored to cater to a range of user requirements. Each plan comes with a specific set of features designed to enhance your experience and meet your study needs. Please find the specifics for each plan below:

<Pricing />
## Bundle

The Bundle Plan offers an all-in-one package that combines the features of the Gameplan and Trainer Plans. The Bundle Plan is priced at €75 per month.
Products included:

- 'Cash-Gameplan'
- 'MTT-Gameplan'
- 'Heads-Up' and
- 'Trainer'

## Gameplan

The Gameplan Plan is designed to enhance your study experience by providing access to the most accurate GTO solutions. The Gameplan Plan is priced at **€49 per month**.

Products included:

- 'Cash-Gameplan'
- 'MTT-Gameplan' and
- 'Heads-Up' spots.

## Trainer

The Trainer Plan is designed to help users train and perfect their skills against our expert GTO bot. The Trainer Plan is priced at €35 per month.
Products included:

- Practice Mode
- Heads-Up spots
- Result tracking

Please note: All charges are billed monthly. If you have any further questions about our plans or billing, we recommend visiting the 'Billing & Subscription' section in our FAQs or reaching out to our support team.

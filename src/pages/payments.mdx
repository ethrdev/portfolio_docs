# Payment methods

We offer two primary methods of payment to subscribe to our plans - PayPal and Credit Card. Here are the specifics:

## PayPal

We accept payments through PayPal. To use this option, select PayPal as your payment method during the checkout process. You'll then be redirected to the PayPal site to complete your transaction.

## Credit Card

We accept credit card payments which are processed securely through Stripe. To use this option, select Credit Card as your payment method and enter your card details. Your information will be handled securely by Stripe, ensuring your transaction is safe.

Please note: Always ensure that your payment information is up-to-date to prevent any service interruptions due to payment failures. If you encounter any issues with payments or need to change your payment method, please reach out to our support team for assistance.
